Announcement "School of Endocrinologist" in Poltava 09/7-8/2017

Scientific and educational project of regional cycle "School of endocrinologist" aims to support scientific development and deepening of the study urgent issues of diagnosis, treatment and prevention of endocrine diseases, and to improve the knowledge and skills in spheres regarding new treatment methods, diagnostics and prevention of pathology of the endocrine system and related diseases. The best scientists and speakers in the field of endocrinology and related specialties were attracted to work in the scientific program "School of endocrinologist". The difference of each School is a multidisciplinary approach to debate priority directions of the various fields of modern endocrinology, interactive communication between audience and speakers, discussions, workshops and master classes. The School would be held on 7-8 September in Poltava.

Scientific and educational project "School of endocrinologist"

Scientific and educational project of regional cycle "School of endocrinologist" aims to support scientific development and deepening of the study urgent issues of diagnosis, treatment and prevention of endocrine diseases, and to improve the knowledge and skills in spheres regarding new treatment methods, diagnostics and prevention of pathology of the endocrine system and related diseases. The best scientists and speakers in the field of endocrinology and related specialties were attracted to work in the scientific program "School of endocrinologist". The difference of each School is a multidisciplinary approach to debate priority directions of the various fields of modern endocrinology, interactive communication between audience and speakers, discussions, workshops and master classes. The project is supported by the Ministry of Health of Ukraine, National Academy of Medical Sciences of Ukraine, the health departments of regional administrations, the Association of Endocrinologists of Ukraine, "the Institute of Endocrinology and Metabolism named by V.P. Komisarenko, National Academy of Medical Sciences of Ukraine, Department of Endocrinology National Medical Academy of Postgraduate Education named by P.L. Shupyk.
