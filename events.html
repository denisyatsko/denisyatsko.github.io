    Scientific and educational project "School of endocrinologist"

    Scientific and educational project of regional cycle "School of endocrinologist" aims to support scientific development and deepening of the study urgent issues of diagnosis, treatment and prevention of endocrine diseases, and to improve the knowledge and skills in spheres regarding new treatment methods, diagnostics and prevention of pathology of the endocrine system and related diseases. The best scientists and speakers in the field of endocrinology and related specialties were attracted to work in the scientific program "School of endocrinologist". The difference of each School is a multidisciplinary approach to debate priority directions of the various fields of modern endocrinology, interactive communication between audience and speakers, discussions, workshops and master classes.
    The project is supported by the Ministry of Health of Ukraine, National Academy of Medical Sciences of Ukraine, the health departments of regional administrations, the Association of Endocrinologists of Ukraine, "the Institute of Endocrinology and Metabolism named by V.P. Komisarenko, National Academy of Medical Sciences of Ukraine, Department of Endocrinology National Medical Academy of Postgraduate Education named by P.L. Shupyk.

    • Sportforum 2014. I Ukrainian founding Scientific Practical Conference "Healthy lifestyles, physical culture, sports. Current issues of Sports Medicine. Rehabilitation: physical, medical, psychological"

    Sport Forum 2014 was successfully held on November 28, 2014 at the Radisson Blu in the center of Kiev. The goal of the project is to improve the health of the Ukrainian nation by implementing a system of rehabilitation measures and sports-patriotic education of youth. Particular attention was paid to considering new approaches and technologies in the diagnosis and treatment of sports and traumatic injuries, as well as improving the rehabilitation of such patients. The main issues were: the main approaches to consulting patients regarding smoking and drinking alcohol, the emergence of cardiovascular diseases in athletes, physical rehabilitation of cardiac patients, the formation of a culture of safety and a healthy lifestyle in the system of sports education in Ukraine. The leading specialists - doctors of sports medicine, therapists, vertebrologists, neurologists, cardiologists, reflexologists, orthopedics-traumatologists, rehabilitation specialists, representatives of local self-government bodies, non-governmental organizations, athletes-were invited to participate in the conference.

    • Workshop training on successful tenders and hospital sales «Love me tender»

    The "Love me tender" training and workshop on successful bidding and hospital sales is regularly held in an open and closed (corporate) format, with emphasis on questions from the customer company. Trainer-speakers are leading recognized experts in the pharmaceutical industry, particularly in the area of its hospital sales. The training program aims to create and develop a platform for discussion and interaction between pharmaceutical business operators, practical familiarity and algorithm for using the electronic public procurement system "ProZorro", as well as improving the system of sales in the hospital segment of the pharmaceutical market of Ukraine. The event takes place in the format of reports, the solution of practical cases (including situational examples from the participants themselves), informal communication and discussions. Post-training support for participants.

    • School of Chief Physician April 25, 2017

    On April 25, 2017, the Chief Physician School was held within the framework of the VI International Medical Congress "Implementation of modern medical science achievements in the practice of health care in Ukraine". The following issues were considered: · Current state and perspectives of development of primary medical care in Ukraine; · Organizational and legal aspects of the activity of the doctor at the PMP level; · Family doctor as a provider of universal and integrated services, interaction with other actors in the health care system, public private partnership, Ukrainian experience; · Creation of a unified electronic medical information exchange system, information and telecommunication technologies, quality management at PMP level; · Implementation of a new financing model, basic principles, financing of diagnostic measures and laboratory research within the PMP; · Reimbursement questions.

    Ukrainian workshop conference "Move towards "ProZorro" in medical branch" 11/4/16

    The All-Ukrainian Forum-Workshop was held in Kyiv within the framework of the educational and practical project "Movement towards "ProZorro"" and became a platform for discussions on the implementation of the electronic procurement system. The speakers talked about their own work experience in the system and methods to overcome the major problems associated with its use. The acute practical questions were answered directly by developers of the system of public e-procurements ProZorro, representatives of the appeal system, the Antimonopoly Committee of Ukraine, commercial sites, health care authorities, business representatives and customers. The motto of the event was "Aware means armed!". The forum has enabled all participants and speakers to share experiences and help accelerate the implementation of healthcare reform and anti-corruption. The main feature of the event was the exchange of experiences, advice and life hacks from market representatives and users of the electronic system "ProZorro", and sharp discussions and frank responses to the Forum participants.

    • Ukrainian specialized workshop conference "Transforming of hospital sales. The move towards «ProZorro» 5/20/16

    The main theme of the All-Ukrainian specialized conference-workshop "Transformation of hospital sales. Movement towards "ProZorro" in the Radisson Blu Podil building has become the topical issue of the gradual introduction of the public e-procurement system "ProZorro" in the area of sales of medicines and medical devices. Discussed the issues of hospital procurement and the prospect of switching to the electronic procurement system "ProZorro" and considered issues of legal support for public procurement and development of rational pharmacotherapy. The participants of the All-Ukrainian Specialized Conference-Workshop were the founders of the Public Procurement System "ProZorro" Maxim Nefodov and Oleksandr Starodubtsev, representatives of public organizations and ministries of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine, pharmaceutical manufacturers, distributors and pharmacy networks, the private and public medical sector, insurance companies, Pharmacists, as well as economic experts. All participants and experts expressed great interest in further meetings to discuss the situation in the hospital sales of Ukraine in a similar format.
